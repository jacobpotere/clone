COMMUNITY INVOLVMENT

It's all about: Love, Hope, & Pizza


Named by Franchise Time Magazines, October 2017


Whether it be a nationally recognized charity or the elementary school down the street, all of us need to give back to deserving charities and our local communities. Since 2009, Hungry Howie’s has supported the National Breast Cancer Foundation® through our one-of-a-kind Love, Hope & Pizza campaign. During the month of October, for every pizza sold in a pink box, we make a donation to the National Breast Cancer Foundation®. Together, we’ve raised over $2.5 million for this deserving cause.

Our independently owned and operated franchisees are part of your community; contact the store nearest you and ask the manager if they can assist you with your local cause.
